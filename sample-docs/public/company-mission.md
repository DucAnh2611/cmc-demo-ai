# Company Mission and Values

Our mission is to make secure, well-governed AI accessible to every enterprise team. We believe the next decade of software will be defined by systems that combine human judgment, organizational context, and trustworthy AI assistance — and that the companies who get the trust layer right will be the ones their customers rely on.

## What we do

We build a platform that helps enterprises deploy AI on top of their existing data and identity infrastructure without compromising compliance posture. Our customers use us to put generative AI in front of internal documentation, support tickets, and engineering knowledge bases while still respecting the access controls their security team configured years ago.

## Our values

- **Trust is earned in lines of code.** Every release should make it easier for a customer's compliance officer to say yes. Documentation, audit trails, and least-privilege defaults are part of the product, not afterthoughts.
- **Default to clarity.** When a teammate or a customer asks a question, the most generous thing we can do is give them a precise answer. Vague communication wastes time on both sides.
- **Own the outcome.** When something breaks, the person closest to it owns the fix end to end — root cause, customer follow-up, postmortem, and the structural change that prevents it next time.
- **Build for the team you'll have, not the team you have.** Code, processes, and documentation should make the next ten engineers as productive as the first ten.
- **Respect the customer's time.** Their business runs on cycles measured in quarters and years, not sprints. We design for stability and predictability.

## How we work together

We are a hybrid team distributed across multiple regions. Important decisions are written down. Reviews are done in writing first, in meetings second. We default to async-friendly communication and keep meetings to the minimum needed for genuine alignment.

If you have feedback on how we work, share it with your manager or post it in `#culture-feedback`. The most useful thing you can do for the company is to push back when we are not living up to these values.
